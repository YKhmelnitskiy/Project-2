Do Violent Video Games Contribute to Gun Violence?

Violent video games have been blamed for school shootings, increases in bullying, and violence towards women. Critics argue that these games desensitize players to violence, reward players for simulating violence, and teach children that violence is an acceptable way to resolve conflicts. We based our analysis on gun violence and video game data that has been collected by authoritative sources to determine if indeed violent video games are the cause of increased gun violence in our country.
