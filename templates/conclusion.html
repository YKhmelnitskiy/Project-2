Project Scope

What makes a good Hotel in Downtown Cleveland?, do national brands have more and/or higher ratings from their Guests?, what are the things that lead a positive polarity in comments? do they represent what customers value the most?. Data analytics allows us now to concentrate, clean, and analyze all types of customer reviews in any industry, which becomes a very powerful and useful tool to evaluate, from the Customer perspective, what matters when it comes to the experience of visiting a Hotel. We are basing our analysis in the reviews collected from Google for the top 20 Hotels in downtown Cleveland, using different data extraction and cleaning, and NLP techniques..
